A Somali Legacy of Care

For centuries, Somali women have gathered under shade trees and inside family homes, sharing recipes and secrets for beauty and longevity. Among the most treasured is the use of fermented rice water—a vitamin-rich ritual that yields incredible hair elasticity, length, and luster.

Ricessence brings this legacy forward. Formulated in honor of the mothers who passed these rituals down, we blend traditional wisdom with modern refinement. Each product uses pure ingredients designed to make your hair care a moment of quiet reverence.
